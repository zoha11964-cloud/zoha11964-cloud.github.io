FAQ

What is compound interest and how does it work?
Compound interest is interest calculated on the initial principal plus accumulated interest from previous periods. Your money grows exponentially over time — the longer you invest, the stronger the compounding effect.
How do I use this compound interest calculator?
Enter your initial investment, annual interest rate, total investment years and monthly contributions. Choose compounding frequency, then click Calculate.
What is the difference between annual and monthly compounding?
Annual compounding calculates interest once per year. Monthly compounding updates interest every month and usually brings higher returns.
Are my personal data stored on your server?
No. All calculations run locally inside your browser. We do not collect any of your data.
Can I use this calculator for retirement planning?
Absolutely. This tool fits retirement savings, education funds and home down payment plans.
Are the calculated results guaranteed?
All results are estimates. Actual returns may vary due to market changes, fees and taxes.
